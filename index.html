Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

kale and quinoa a healthy base for reasted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time :45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken untill golden brown and 165 in middle.

    Time: 40 minutes

  2. Toss quinoa chicken, kale and Caesar Dressing untill coated.

    Time: 4 minutes

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish.

    Time: 1 minutes
